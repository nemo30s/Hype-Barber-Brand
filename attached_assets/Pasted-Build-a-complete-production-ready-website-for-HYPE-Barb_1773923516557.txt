Build a complete, production-ready website for HYPE Barber — a barbershop and concept store located at Klapdorp 24, Antwerp, Belgium.
Brand & Aesthetic
The vibe is black and white streetwear editorial — think Supreme meets a luxury barbershop. Dark, cinematic, cultural. The brand has a strong Black culture identity without being exclusive — the tagline is "All hair types, all backgrounds." The logo is an illustrated cartoon of two barbers (one cutting the other's hair), with an ornate sign reading "HYPE BARBER" — it's playful, confident, and street (i'll provide the logo). Use it prominently. Color palette: pure black, off-white #f2ede4, with maybe a single accent used sparingly for CTAs, underlines, hover states. No gold, no purple gradients, no generic AI aesthetics.
Typography
Pair Bebas Neue (Google Fonts) for all display headings — large, condensed, uppercase, very tight leading — with DM Sans (300 weight) for body text. Headings should be oversized and editorial, like a magazine cover. Use Playfair Display italic for the occasional accent word inside headings.
Hero Section
Full viewport height. Use a looping background video of a barbershop (use a direct Pexels MP4 source or a similar free CDN-hosted barbershop video). Add a dark overlay so text is legible. Animate the headline letter by letter or line by line on load. Headline: "YOUR / STYLE. / ELEVATED." — third line in teal. Sub: "Antwerp's  barbershop and concept store." Two CTAs: "Book Appointment" (teal filled button) and "Explore Services" (ghost outline button). Add a subtle scroll indicator on the right side.
Animations & Interactions

Animated film grain overlay across the entire page (CSS + SVG noise, very subtle)
Sticky nav that becomes frosted glass (backdrop-filter blur) on scroll
Every section animates in on scroll with IntersectionObserver (fade up + translateY)
Service cards: gold underline slides in from left on hover, background shifts to slightly lighter black
Gallery images: grayscale by default, color on hover with subtle scale
Count-up animation on stats (160+ reviews, 4.8★)
Parallax depth on hero overlay on scroll
