## Despre

Hype Barber is a unisex Hair Salon located in Antwerp, offering services for both men and women. In addition to our salon services, we also retail a curated selection of high-quality hair products and accessories. Conveniently situated at Klapdorp 24, 2000 Antwerp, our aim is to cultivate a warm and inviting atmosphere where every customer feels at ease and welcomed. We strive to transform the typical salon visit into an unforgettable experience, ensuring that each client leaves feeling not only satisfied with their hair but also rejuvenated by the entire process.
